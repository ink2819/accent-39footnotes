Accent Sisters 重音社

About the Exhibition
Presented as the inaugural exhibition at Accent Sisters’ new location in NYC’s Union Square, 39 Footnotes is a spatial verse co-authored by 30+ visual artists, novelists, writers, musicians, filmmakers, furniture designers, and other creative minds from various media, alongside the three project initiators—Qianfan Gu, Jiaoyang Li, and Na Zhong.
